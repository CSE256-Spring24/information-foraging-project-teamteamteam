General Schedule

There are two types of classes: Morning and Afternoon classes. Bear Tracks, an extension class, is available for both classes.

If you opt into bear tracks, the longest period of time your child can be in class is around 5 and a half hours.

Morning Class Schedule

8:50 – 11:45 a.m. Morning Class
11:45 a.m. – 2:30 p.m. Bear Tracks

Afternoon Class Schedule

9:45 a.m. – 12:30 p.m. Bear Tracks
12:30 p.m. – 3:15 p.m. Afternoon Class
For more specifics about individual class schedules, please visit their respective pages.
